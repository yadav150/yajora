About Yajora

Fresh, local, and delivered with care — from our family to yours.

Our Story

Yajora was born from a simple yet powerful belief: everyone deserves access to fresh, high-quality groceries without the hassle. What started as a small neighbourhood initiative in Hawapur West, Karbi Anglong, Assam, has grown into a trusted online grocery delivery platform serving families across the region.

We partner directly with local farms, trusted suppliers, and artisanal producers to bring you the freshest produce, pantry staples, dairy products, and everyday essentials — all at fair prices. Every item is carefully selected to meet our quality standards, ensuring that what you receive is nothing short of excellent.

Our Mission

To make fresh, healthy food accessible to every household by building a reliable, transparent, and community-driven grocery delivery service that prioritizes quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction above all else.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted grocery partner in Northeast India — known for freshness, integrity, and a deep commitment to the communities we serve.

Our Values

🌱 Freshness First

We source directly from farms to ensure peak freshness and flavour.

🤝 Community Trust

We build lasting relationships with customers, farmers, and suppliers.

🌍 Sustainability

We support eco-friendly farming practices and reduce food waste.

❤️ Quality Guarantee

We stand behind every product we deliver — 100% satisfaction guaranteed.
